## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**DEGEN ACADEMY** is a browser-based idle/strategy game that teaches real DeFi (Decentralized Finance) mechanics through engaging gameplay. Players learn about yield farming, rug pulls, halvings, gas fees, and market dynamics - all without risking real money.

### The Opportunity
- 420M+ people hold cryptocurrency, but <5% understand DeFi mechanics
- $50B+ lost to DeFi exploits/rugs - education is desperately needed
- Idle games market: $14B (Cookie Clicker, Adventure Capitalist genre)
- No one has combined "DeFi education" with "addictive idle gameplay"

### The Product
A free-to-play game where players:
1. Deposit virtual tokens in yield pools
2. Earn rewards over time (idle mechanic)
3. Survive random rug pulls, exploits, and market chaos
4. Learn real DeFi concepts through gameplay
5. Graduate with a certificate after mastering all mechanics

### Business Model
- F2P core with Battle Pass ($5/mo)
- Cosmetics marketplace ($1-10)
- B2B education licenses ($500-5000/yr)
- Protocol sponsorships ($1K-10K/mo)

### Why Now
- Crypto market recovering, new wave of curious users incoming
- DeFi education demand at all-time high post-FTX
- Idle games are having a renaissance (Banana clicker, etc.)
- AI enables personalized coaching (Ralph the AI mascot)

## 5. DETAILED FEATURE SPEC

### 5.1 MVP Scope (Day 1 Build)

**Must Have (P0):**
- [ ] 6 yield pools with different APYs
- [ ] Click to deposit/withdraw tokens
- [ ] Idle yield accumulation (ticks every second)
- [ ] Halving timer (every 5 minutes, yields -50%)
- [ ] RNG rug pulls (15% chance per minute)
- [ ] Basic portfolio balance display
- [ ] Win condition: Reach $1M
- [ ] Lose condition: Reach $0
- [ ] LocalStorage save

**Should Have (P1 - Week 1):**
- [ ] Audit purchase system (blocks exploits)
- [ ] Insurance system (softens whale dumps)
- [ ] 5 RNG event types (rug, exploit, whale, gas, pump)
- [ ] Sound effects (lo-fi, alarms, sad violin)
- [ ] Death screen with meme text
- [ ] One-tap Twitter share button

- [ ] Ralph AI coach comments
- [ ] Leaderboard (speedrun times)
- [ ] Achievement system
- [ ] Multiple game modes
- [ ] Historical event scenarios

**Won't Have (P3 - Future):**

- [ ] Multiplayer
- [ ] Mobile native apps
