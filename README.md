## How to Play

### Objective
Start with **$10,000** and grow your portfolio to **$1,000,000** to graduate!

### Mechanics

**Yield Farming**
- Deposit into 6 different pools with varying APYs (8% - 420%)
- Higher APY = Higher risk of getting rugged
- Yields accumulate every second

**Halving**
- Every 5 minutes, all yields are cut in half
- Plan your strategy around halving cycles!

### Controls
- Click **+$1K** to deposit $1,000 into a pool
- Click **OUT** to withdraw all funds from a pool
- Buy items in the bottom bar
